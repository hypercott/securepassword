Secure Password Generator: Privacy Policy

 

We don't collect any personal data. We do not store any of the auto-generated passwords and do not store information that could associate passwords with internet addresses (IP addresses) that requested their generation. We do not use cookies on this site, however thrid-party vendors may (see below).

Our website also includes code from traffic trackers that provide general information and statistics based on your IP address. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt-out of Google personalized advertising by visiting https://www.google.com/settings/ads.
